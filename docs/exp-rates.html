Valius uses a layered experience system. Every XP drop passes through several multipliers applied in sequence — base rate, total-level scaling, stacking bonuses, and finally your game mode multiplier. The sections below cover each layer, with the most impactful system first.

Total Level Scaling

⚡ Total Level XP Scaling Current System

The higher your total level, the larger the multiplier applied to every XP drop. This rewards long-term progression — the closer you are to max, the faster remaining skills train. The multiplier is looked up live each XP drop from your current total level, so it increases automatically as you level up.

Below total level 500 no scaling bonus applies. Once you hit 500 the multiplier kicks in and grows through eight tiers up to 210× at max total level (2277).

Tier-up notification: When a level-up pushes your total level across a tier threshold, you receive a coloured chat message — e.g. "[XP Scaling] Total level 500 reached! Tier: Bronze — XP multiplier now 25x." The new multiplier applies from that XP drop onward.

This multiplier stacks on top of the base rate and all other bonuses. A Normal mode player woodcutting at Bronze tier already receives 30 × 25 = 750× OSRS rates before any events, scrolls, or gear bonuses.

Flat Rate System Previous System

Previously all players received the same fixed multiplier regardless of total level. There was no scaling — a fresh account and a near-max account trained at identical rates. The only differences came from game mode, events, gear, and scrolls.

The base rates (30× skilling, 125× combat) and all stacking bonuses below still exist in the current system — total level scaling is simply an additional multiplier layered on top.

Worked Examples

Fresh account (total level < 500) — Normal mode, skilling, no bonuses
Raw OSRS XP × 30 = 30× OSRS rates
Bronze tier (total 500+) — Normal mode, Woodcutting, on a Saturday
Raw × 30 (base) × 25 (bronze scaling) × 2 (weekend) = Raw × 1,500
Gold tier (total 1000+) — Extreme mode, Agility, Tier 4 diary item
Raw × 30 (base) × 60 (gold scaling) × 1.20 (diary) × 15 (extreme) = Raw × 32,400
Max total (2277) — Normal mode, all server events active, vote bonus active
Raw × 30 × 210 (max scaling) × 2 (weekend) × 1.5 (WoGW) × 2 (event) × 1.2 (vote) = Raw × 45,360

Experience Cap

Each skill has a hard cap of 200,000,000 XP. On hitting the cap:

  • 40 Loyalty Points are deposited automatically.
  • A server-wide announcement broadcasts the achievement.
  • No further XP is awarded in that skill.
Developer note: Total-level tier thresholds and multipliers are the TOTAL_LEVEL_THRESHOLDS and TOTAL_LEVEL_BONUSES arrays in src/valius/Config.java. Edit those arrays to rebalance tiers without touching logic. The lookup runs in PlayerAssistant.getTotalLevelBonus() and is applied inside addSkillXP() just before the game mode multiplier.
