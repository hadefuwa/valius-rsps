Overview

Blood Money is the primary Player-vs-Player reward currency in Valius. It is earned by defeating other players — primarily in the Wilderness and the PK District — and can be exchanged at a dedicated shop for powerful custom equipment unavailable elsewhere.

Blood Money is carried as a stackable item (ID: 13307) in your inventory. It can also be stored in the Safe Box — it is the only item withdrawable from the Safe Box outside of clan wars safe mode, making it safe to transport out of dangerous zones.

NPC Location

NPC: Renegade Knight  NPC ID 4321

Location: Home teleport area ~3090, 3500

Shop tab: 4th tab of the Point Reward Shops interface
  1. Teleport Home using the spellbook or ::home command.
  2. Locate the Point Reward Shops NPC cluster near the bank.
  3. Click the Renegade Knight (or right-click → Shop).
  4. Navigate to the 4th tab — "Blood Money" — in the tabbed shop interface.

The Point Reward Shops NPC opens a tabbed interface with four reward shops:

Notes

  • Blood Money can be stored in the Safe Box — it is the only item withdrawable outside of clan wars safe mode, making it useful for safely moving earnings out of dangerous zones.
  • Purchasing additional Safe Deposit Box slots costs 15 Blood Money per extra slot.
  • Talking to the Renegade Knight shows your current Blood Money balance in the chat.
  • The shop does not allow selling items back (the Can Sell To flag is disabled in the shop definition).
  • Blood Money stacks in your inventory as item ID 13307.
