What Is a Donator Token?

The primary premium currency on Valius

Donator Tokens (item ID 8800) are purchased with real money through the website donation store. They can be spent in the in-game Donator Token Shop for cosmetics, mystery boxes, weapons, and more. Accumulating donation dollars also permanently raises your donator rank, which unlocks commands, XP zone bonuses, PKP boosts, and other perks regardless of how you spend the tokens.

Key mechanics

Tokens vs. amDonated Tokens are the spendable currency you receive in your inventory. amDonated is your cumulative dollar total — it never decreases and determines your permanent rank. Spending tokens in the shop does not reduce amDonated.
Rank update timing Your rank is recalculated via player.updateRank() every time tokens are deducted in the shop, so a rank upgrade takes effect immediately after a purchase that crosses a threshold.
Ranks are permanent Once a tier is reached it is locked in permanently — even if donation records were somehow altered.
Obtaining Tokens

Real-money donations are the primary source. Several in-game sources also exist.

When tokens are delivered on claim, a bonus percentage is added on top of the purchased amount based on the total value of that donation.

Special skin colours

Requirement Players with amDonated ≥ $10 unlock three exclusive skin colours (IDs 8, 9, 10) in the character appearance interface. Below $10 the client shows: "You must be a donator to use these skin colors."
Donator Token Shop

Shop ID 9 · Currency: Donator Token (item 8800) · One-way exchange — tokens cannot be sold back

How spending works When you buy an item, tokens are deleted from your inventory and player.updateRank() is called immediately — so crossing a donation threshold via the shop upgrades your rank on the spot. Items cannot be sold back (sell modifier is for display only).
